## Decisions Made

- **Single-statement UPDATE over `sql.transaction([...])`** — research recommended this approach because it's one round-trip vs two, simpler, no driver swap, no migration. PostgreSQL MVCC + ROW EXCLUSIVE lock for the duration of one statement provides the same atomicity guarantee.
- **Bounded WHERE clause** — `or(eq(active,true), eq(id,newId))` instead of an unbounded `WHERE TRUE`. Avoids a full-table rewrite per promote (research: meaningful difference after 30+ days of snapshots).
- **Drop `and` from imports** — grep confirmed line 475 (the line being replaced) was the only `and(` use site. Final import: `{ eq, or, sql }`.
- **Diff-hook untouched** — Per research Pitfall 6, REL-01 scope is exclusively the snapshot promotion.
