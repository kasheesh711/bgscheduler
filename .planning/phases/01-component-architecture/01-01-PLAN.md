<objective>
Anchor the DB and SearchIndex singletons on `globalThis` so they survive Next.js dev-mode HMR module reloads.

Purpose: Module-level `let` variables are reset when Next.js reloads modules during development. This causes unnecessary DB reconnections and expensive SearchIndex rebuilds. Anchoring on `globalThis` is the standard Next.js pattern for dev-mode singleton persistence (per D-07).

Output: Two modified files with `globalThis` anchoring, zero behavioral change in production.
</objective>
