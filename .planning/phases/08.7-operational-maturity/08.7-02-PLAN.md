| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-08.7-06 | Spoofing | `/api/data-health` fetch | accept | Existing route requires Auth.js session; unauthenticated fetch renders no banner. |
| T-08.7-07 | Denial of Service | Banner polling | mitigate | Single mount-time fetch; no polling or retries. |

</threat_model>
